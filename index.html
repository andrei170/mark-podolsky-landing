THE REAL ESTATE PROBLEM

Most "Passive Income" Real Estate
Is Not Actually Passive

Rental houses, short-term rentals, flipping. All three come with tenants, repairs, property managers who don't manage, and midnight phone calls. Vacant land does none of that.

KILLER 01

Tenants + Termites

Rental real estate breaks when a furnace dies at 2 a.m. or an eviction drags on for six months. Land has no tenants, no repairs, no maintenance calls.

KILLER 02

Thin Cash Flow

After mortgage, insurance, taxes, and management, most rentals cash-flow $150 a month. Land sold on terms cash-flows hundreds per parcel with no expenses.

KILLER 03

Capital-Heavy Entry

A rental in a growing metro costs $300K+ and 20% down. A raw parcel costs $500 to $5,000 cash. The barrier to entry is almost zero.

THE METHOD

The 4-Step Land Geek System,
Run Over 5,500 Times

STEP 1

Find Motivated Sellers

County tax-delinquent lists, out-of-state owners, inherited property lists. Owners who don't want the land and are waiting for someone to take it off their hands.

STEP 2

Offer 25-30 Cents

Direct mail at 25 to 30 cents on the dollar. High volume, predictable response. The math works because most sellers are burnt out on paying taxes.

STEP 3

Close Cheap + Fast

Most parcels close in under two weeks through a title company or a simple notarized deed. Paperwork is templated inside Flight School.

STEP 4

Resell On Terms

List at 3x to 10x what you paid. Take a down payment, finance the rest for 60-120 months. You become the bank. Monthly income. Zero maintenance.
